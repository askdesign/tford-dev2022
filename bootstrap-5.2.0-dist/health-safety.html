HEALTH AND SAFETY

Our Safety Program

T Ford Company, Inc. considers the health and safety of our employees, subcontractors and the public to be paramount to the success of each project and our company.

T Ford Company, Inc. maintains a vigorous Safety program under the direct oversight of Jack Enos, a principal of TFCI. The components of our program include the following:

  • Safety Training for all field staff
  • 40-Hour Hazwoper
  • 8-Hour Annual Refresher
  • OSHA 10 Hour Construction Worker
  • Weekly Toolbox Talks
  • Daily Job Safety Briefs
  • Random Safety Audits
  • Site Specific Health & Safety Plans
  • Fleet Safety Program
  • Medical Monitoring
  • Random Drug and Alcohol Testing

The most important component of our safety program is our staff. An experienced staff with a low turnover rate has enabled us to maintain an EMR of less than 1.00 ten out of the last eleven years. In addition T Ford Company, Inc. has been awarded Bronze, Silver, and Gold STEP Safety Awards by the Associated Builders and Contractors.
